Newton-Raphon Method

Mon Aug 29, 2022

Introduction

  1. Newton-Raphson Method is a must for every power engineer to write down the equation for a NR 3-Bus, 3 unknown system.
  2. This is once-in-a-life-time activity.

Topology and Parameter

  • Generating bus on 1-bus and 3-bus.
  • power demand: $S_{L}$

Objective

  • Gvien data on 3-bus system, determine unknown states of system NR Method.

Solution

Step 1

known states = [$\delta_{1}$ $V_{1}$ $V_{3}$ ]

unknown states = [$\delta_{2}$ $\delta_{3}$ $V_{2}$]

为什么选择下面三个式子？

corresponding PowerFlow equation: [$P_{2}$ $P_{3}$ $Q_{2}$]

Step 2

Build Y-bus

$y_{12} = -j10$

$y_{23} = -j5$

$y_{13} = -j4$

*theta represents imaginary part of y.

Step 3

According to Newton-Raphson Load flow :

$P_{k} = \sum_{i=1}^{n}\left | Y_{ki}V_{i}V_{k} \right |cos(\theta_{ki}+\delta_{i}-\delta_{k})$

$Q_{k} = -\sum_{i=1}^{n}\left | Y_{ki}V_{i}V_{k} \right |sin(\theta_{ki}+\delta_{i}-\delta_{k})$

We have:

$f_{p2} = \left | Y_{21}V_{1}V_{2} \right |cos(\theta_{21}+\delta_{1}-\delta_{2}) + \left | Y_{22}V_{2}V_{2} \right | cos(\theta_{22})+ \ \left |Y_{23}V_{3}V_{2} \right | cos(\theta_{23}+\delta_{3}-\delta_{2}) - P_{2} = 0 $

$f_{p3} = \left | Y_{31}V_{1}V_{2} \right |cos(\theta_{31}+\delta_{1}-\delta_{3}) + \left | Y_{32}V_{2}V_{3} \right | cos(\theta_{32}+\delta_{2}-\delta_{3})+ \left |Y_{33}(V_{3})^{2} \right | cos(\theta_{33}) - P_{3} = 0$

$f_{Q2} = -\left | Y_{21}V_{1}V_{2} \right |sin(\theta_{21}+\delta_{1}-\delta_{2}) - \left | Y_{22}(V_{2})^2 \right | sin(\theta_{22}) - \ \left |Y_{23}V_{3}V_{2} \right | sin(\theta_{23} + \delta_{3}-\delta_{2}) - Q_{2} = 0$
